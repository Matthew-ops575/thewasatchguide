Finding a dentist you actually want to go back to is one of those small quality-of-life things that matters more than it should. Sandy has a deep bench of dental practices along the 700 East and 9400 South corridors — everything from long-running family offices to pediatric specialists who know how to keep a four-year-old calm in the chair. Here are the practices that locals keep recommending to each other.

## The High-Volume Family Practice

**Fort Union Family Dental** (8915 S 700 East) has been a south valley anchor for years, and the numbers tell the story — nearly 500 Google reviews at a 4.9 average, which is unusual for any dental office. Dr. Todd Larsen, Dr. Timothy Anger, and Dr. Marie Peterson run a practice built around clear communication; patients consistently mention that procedures are explained thoroughly before anything starts. They were named a top-three finalist for Best of SLC Magazine in 2025. Hours run Monday through Thursday with early Friday availability, which helps if you're trying to squeeze in a cleaning before work. Best for families who want a well-oiled, high-confidence operation where nothing feels rushed.

## The Husband-and-Wife Team

**Taylor Dental Care** (9924 S 700 East) is run by Drs. Glenn and Michelle Taylor, and the family-practice feel is genuine — they've been at it since the mid-nineties. Glenn graduated from the University of Oklahoma College of Dentistry and the two of them handle everything from routine cleanings to cosmetic work and pediatric visits. The office runs Monday through Thursday and closes on Fridays. Reviews lean heavily on the "they treat you like a person, not a chart number" theme, and the 5.0 rating on US News with 39 reviews backs that up. Best for patients who prefer seeing the same dentist every time and value a smaller, relationship-driven practice.

## The Pediatric Specialist With a Following

**Bright Pediatric Dentistry** (1434 E 9400 South, Suite 205) is Dr. David Anderson's practice, and his background is unusual — he was a fifth-grade teacher before becoming a board-certified pediatric dentist. That shows up in the way the office runs: kids pick their own Netflix or Disney+ show on a ceiling-mounted TV during cleanings, choose from flavored toothpaste options, and leave with a token for the toy machine. Dr. Erick Lund handles orthodontics in the same office. The practice carries a 5.0 rating across multiple platforms with over 400 reviews total. Best for young kids, anxious first-timers, and parents who want a dentist whose entire practice is designed around children.

## The Dual-Board-Certified Kids' Office

**kidgrin — Creek Road Orthodontics & Pediatric Dentistry** (7410 S Creek Road, Suite 200) is Dr. Barney Olsen's practice, and his credentials set him apart: board-certified in both pediatric dentistry and orthodontics, which means kids can get braces and dental work under one roof without referrals. Dr. Olsen also treats children with special needs, including sedation dentistry for patients who need it and hospital-based care for more complex cases. The Creek Road location is slightly west of the main Sandy commercial corridors, which means parking is easy and the office doesn't feel like it's wedged into a strip mall. Best for families who want pediatric and orthodontic care combined, and for children with special needs.

## The Modern Cosmetic Office

**Platinum Dental of Sandy** (1844 E 9400 South) sits at the east end of the 9400 South corridor and runs a schedule that's genuinely accommodating — open until 7 p.m. on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays, with Saturday hours from 9 to 3. That alone separates it from most dental offices in Sandy. The practice handles the full range of general and cosmetic services — veneers, crowns, whitening, implants — and carries a 4.8 rating across 500-plus reviews. A small number of patients have flagged billing transparency as an issue, so it's worth confirming costs upfront if you're doing anything beyond a routine visit. Best for working professionals who need evening or weekend availability, and anyone shopping for cosmetic work.

## The Long-Running Sandy Original

**Sandy Center Family and Cosmetic Dentistry** (8941 S 700 East, Suite 101) is Dr. Nicholas LaFeber's practice, and he's been at it for nearly two decades. The office handles a wide scope — implants, Invisalign, TMJ treatment, root canals, cosmetic enhancements — which means you're less likely to get referred out for specialty work. Hours are Monday through Wednesday with a Thursday half-day, and the office closes for the weekend. The practice isn't the flashiest on this list, but it's the kind of place where you make one appointment, they handle everything, and you don't think about it again until the next cleaning reminder shows up. Best for adults who want a one-stop general and cosmetic practice with an experienced hand.

## The South Sandy Option

**Granite View Dental** (1030 E 11400 South, Suite 2) sits at Sandy's southern edge and serves as the go-to for residents in the 11400 South corridor who don't want to drive north for everything. Dr. Will Ellingson runs the practice with a focus on preventive, cosmetic, restorative, and implant work. Reviews consistently mention a clean, modern office and thorough cleanings — several patients have called it the most comprehensive cleaning they've had. The office also offers pediatric services, making it a workable option for families who want one location for everyone. Best for south Sandy and near-Draper residents who want a modern, thorough general practice close to home.

## The Full-Service Group Practice

**Abundant Dental Care of Sandy** (641 W 9000 South) is the largest-footprint operation on this list, with extended hours (7 a.m. to 7 p.m. weekdays, Saturday mornings) and five specialties under one roof: general, cosmetic, emergency, orthodontics, and pediatric. That breadth is the draw — if your kid needs a filling at 7 a.m. before school or you crack a tooth on a Saturday morning, they can see you. The practice carries a 4.4 rating across 500-plus reviews. The scale means you may not always see the same dentist, which matters to some patients and doesn't to others. Best for anyone who prioritizes convenience, extended hours, and same-day emergency availability.

## The Neighborhood Favorite

**Quarry Bend Dental** (9141 S Village Shop Drive) is Dr. Gregory Randall's practice, tucked into the Village Shop plaza off 9000 South. Dr. Randall carries a 5.0 on Zocdoc with high marks for bedside manner, and the office has won Nextdoor's Neighborhood Favorite designation multiple years running. The schedule is unconventional — closed Monday and Tuesday, open Wednesday through Saturday — which works well for some patients and poorly for others. Reviews repeatedly use the word "honest," which in dentistry usually means the dentist isn't pushing unnecessary work. Best for patients who've had bad experiences elsewhere and want a low-pressure, trust-first relationship with their dentist.

Sandy's dental landscape mirrors the city itself: established, practical, and deeper than it looks from the outside. Whether you need a pediatric specialist who can keep a toddler calm, a cosmetic office with Saturday hours, or a family practice where the dentist knows your name, the options here are strong enough that you shouldn't have to drive to another city for care.
